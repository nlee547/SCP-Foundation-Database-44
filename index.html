Audio Log 6632 

[Audio Log: Research Facility, SCP-XXXX] 

[DOOR OPENS] 

Dr. Samantha Hayes: [Clears throat] Uh, this is Dr. Samantha Hayes, SCP Research Team Lead. Today's date is April 17th, 7854. Uh, we've received a new SCP designation, SCP-XXXX, and I'm here to log our initial findings. 

[Sound of footsteps approaching] 

Dr. Hayes: [Slightly startled] Oh, come in! 

[Door opens] 

Dr. Hayes: Oh, it's just you, Jason. What do you need? 

Jason: Sorry to interrupt, Dr. Hayes. I just wanted to drop off the latest lab results on SCP-XXXX. 

Dr. Hayes: Perfect timing, actually. I was just about to discuss our findings. Please, have a seat. 

[Sound of a chair being pulled] 

Jason: Thanks, Dr. Hayes. 

Dr. Hayes: Alright, let's get started. SCP-XXXX appears to be a metallic box, approximately 30 centimeters in length, 20 centimeters in width, and 15 centimeters in height. It's locked tight, and initial attempts to open it have been unsuccessful. 

Jason: What's inside the box? 

Dr. Hayes: That's what we're trying to figure out. There's something in there, but our scans aren't showing anything conclusive. It's like the contents are shielded from detection. 

Jason: Mysterious. 

Dr. Hayes: Indeed. However, there's something else peculiar about this SCP. During our initial examination, we discovered faint traces of fluorescent material on the surface. 

Jason: Fluorescent material? 

Dr. Hayes: Yes, it's barely visible under regular light, but when exposed to UV light... 

[Sound of a brief pause] 

Jason: UV light? 

Dr. Hayes: Yes, UV light. It's as if there's something hidden within the box that can only be revealed under UV illumination. I've ordered a UV flashlight to further investigate this anomaly. 

Jason: Fascinating. Do you think this hidden element could hold the key to unlocking the mysteries of SCP-XXXX? 

Dr. Hayes: It's possible. We won't know for sure until we get our hands on that flashlight and shine some light on this enigma. 

Dr. Hayes: Anyways, do you mind grabbing that SD card from the dark room?  

Jason: Of course, Dr. Hayes. I'll be right back. 

[Sound of footsteps receding] 

[Audio Log ends]
